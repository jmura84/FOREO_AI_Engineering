HOW TO USE

Simple & easy

STEP 1

Put on mask

Start with a clean and dry face & apply FAQTM P1, if desired. Then place your FAQTM 202 Silicone LED Face Mask over your face, and secure in place with the headband.

STEP 2

Turn on

Press the universal button to turn on your mask. You can change the LED colors by quick-pressing the button again. With FAQTM 202, you can access more settings and pre-programmed treatments via the FAQTM Swiss app. Download the app here.

STEP 3

Move freely

Enjoy your LED facial for up to 15 mins, while you continue with your day - for multitasking at its best. Once finished, press the universal button for 3 seconds to turn off your mask.

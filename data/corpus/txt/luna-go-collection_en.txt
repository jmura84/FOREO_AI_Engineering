Simple & easy
STEP 1
Unlock device
Unlock device by holding down the + and - buttons for 3 seconds. Then register your warranty via the FOREO For You app. Download the app here.
STEP 2
Apply cleanser
Dampen your face and apply your face cleanser. For optimal results, use FOREO Micro-Foam Cleanser. Wet LUNATM 4 go and press the universal button to turn on the device.
STEP 3
Cleanse
Adjust the intensity of the T-SonicTM pulsations with the + and - buttons. Glide the front of the device in circular motions around your face and neck. Rinse face/neck and device.
STEP 4
Firm
Apply FOREO's Serum. Press the universal button to turn on device. Then gently glide back of device in straight upward lines across your face and neck for a firming massage.

Full-spectrum vs. traditional red light
Traditional red light therapy devices have tried to replicate the benefits we get from the sun, using single LED wavelengths (e.g. 650 nm, 850 nm). And while these wavelengths have shown clinically proven results - their effects are limited. Because the sun doesn’t emit a single wavelength - it delivers a broad, rich spectrum of red light. FAQ™ 502 is the first at-home device to use both broad-spectrum and narrow wavelength light technologies. This is what allows it to optimize results across multiple skin layers, and at precise depths - just like the sun (without the harmful UV and blue light).

Simple & easy
STEP 1
Prep
Before starting your treatment, skin should be clean and dry, and you should put on the protective glasses. To optimize your treatment even further, apply FAQ™ Red Light Peptide Serum onto the areas you wish to treat, and gently massage until fully absorbed.
STEP 2
Select intensity
Press the universal button to turn on your FAQ™ 502 device. Then adjust the intensity by pressing the + and - buttons. You can access video-guided treatments and more settings via the FAQ™ Swiss app. Download app
STEP 3
Rejuvenate
Lightly place the silicone shield to your skin. For large areas, press and hold the flash button while gliding on skin. For small areas, press and immediately release the flash button.
